The Transfiguration of Saudi Arabia

This morning I came across an interesting article on the reopening of cinemas and public concerts in Saudi Arabia and I'm excited. To be honest, if I was asked what countries did not have cinemas I would probably have guessed Saudi Arabia (they sort of have a reputation lol) but the thought never crossed my mind that there were countries which still banned cinemas. In 2018. Anyway, the Crown Prince Muhammad who ascended the throne late last year has implemented a number of reforms, of which the reopening of cinemas is one. Prince Muhammad is really on a wave, trying to be progressive and all. He's there letting women drive, getting them better jobs and so much more. I guess its safe to say that things are getting better for the average Saudi Arabian. At least thats what it looks like from the outside.

Back to our cinema gist. Guess what movie is going to be the first movie shown when cinemas open. Yep! thats right, Black Panther. So a young Monarch who just ascended the throne and is trying to transform his country lifts a cinema ban and the first movie to be shown is about a young monarch who just ascended the throne and is trying to transform his country. hmm,nice.
